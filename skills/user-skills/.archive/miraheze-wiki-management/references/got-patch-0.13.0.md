- **Nocturnal Twilight** — CrawlerTV's Liquid Death removed
- **Ought Be Damned** — Sui Dabz Blow Torch removed
- **Blackout Files Pt1** — Sui Dabz Blow Torch removed

Players with active quests containing these items must abandon and re-accept them.

## Gameplay Changes

- Full Wipe — All player progress reset
- Boss reintroduction schedule:
  - Week 1: Krtek (Missile Silo) only
  - Week 2: Krtek + The Collector (Matka Miest)
  - Week 3: All bosses return to Tabor
  - May 15, 2026: Mall bosses Nikolai & Tatra returned to Chodov Mall
  - All bosses active as of May 15, 2026
- Foxtrot armor rebalanced
- Market pricing rebalanced
- Mag purchase limit increased
- Ration system improvements
- Ammo loot balanced for more variations in raid
