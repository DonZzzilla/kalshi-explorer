## Discord Intel (BOA Hub Q&A Forum — 43 threads reviewed)

### Boss Strategy
- **Collector (Matka):** Funnel through doorways; guards + boss sprayed through single doorway. AI pathing exploit at bottom door — agro from bunks, wait for footsteps to stop, peek when stuck.
- **Mall Bosses:** Tatra spawns in back of top floor Gappo and food court. Nikolai spawns in back left of tech store and X insurance (near purple vault). Both roam toward gunfire. Nikolai runs around when out of bullets.
- **Boss Loot Rankings:** Mamba best armor; Collector best helmet+gun; Tatra good armor+gun; Krtek good all-around.

### Loot/Map
- **Missile Silo:** Best map for keycards (easiest to loot), though all maps are RNG
- **Green Vault (Matka):** Best vault for AP ammo pouches
- **AP Ammo:** "FMJ tickles anything above T5" — need AP for high-tier armor

### Trader/Quest
- **Shiro Questline:** Gives 35 bottles of AP — recommended for AP farming
- **Task/Quest Stacking:** Stack tasks with quests/map objectives to maximize rep/XP
- **Keycard Farming:** Go mall naked with Ruger, headtap nearest Foxtrot, check all spawns

### PvP/Strategy
- **Foxtrots:** "Foxtrots are more dangerous than players" — deadly when they gang up
- **Without AP ammo:** Avoid distance engagements; play stealthy, use cover
