## Money Making Guide — Full Thread Transcript

**Thread:** "Money issues" (28 replies)
**Tags:** General (Unsure)

### Question (RALYK$):
So I recently just bought a delta off a person for the price in the bm GoT and spent around all my money, I wanna know the best method to get money as of april

### BOA Mentor Answers:

1. Missions help a lot — Finding valuable items like boss figures and running keycards/flares are also good ways.
2. Hourly mission reset — They are now hourly, and as long as you don't die immediately, they can be useful for farming.
3. Trader quests — I would recommend doing quests for traders and doing easy tasks that give you a decent amount of money.
4. Budget management — Also don't spend all your money on something if you don't need to. You can always wait to get a delta.
5. Sell unused inventory — Also sell stuff you may have laying around you won't use. For example, all the food you get from rations.
6. Finding teammates — Training raids or just don't play alone. The official ghosts of tabor discord has a channel to find people.
7. LFG etiquette — That's not true, 80% I've met on there are super good people. Nobody team kills, and most people go along the lines of "your vault/flare, your loot first."
8. Lobby strategy — You could also just wait in a lobby for awhile and run it then, but that runs the risk of others doing the same.

### Wiki-Ready Summary (Money Making Methods — as of April 2026)

| Method | Details | Risk Level |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Trader quests | Easy tasks that give decent money | Low |
| Hourly missions | Reset every hour; farmable if you survive | Medium |
| Keycards & Flares | High-value loot from vault runs | Medium-High |
| Boss figures | Find and sell for profit | Medium |
| Sell unused items | Food from rations, extra gear | Low |
| LFG teammates | Official GoT Discord; ~80% positive rate | Low-Medium |

### Key Etiquette Notes
- "Your vault/flare, your loot first" — standard etiquette in group raids
- Wait in lobby for others to thin out before doing vault runs
- Training raids help with fear of playing alone
