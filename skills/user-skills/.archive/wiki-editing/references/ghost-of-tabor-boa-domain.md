# Ghost of Tabor / BOA Domain Knowledge

For wiki editing context on boa.miraheze.org and got.miraheze.org.

## Core Identity Facts

- **BOA** = Battlefield Observation & Awareness — a group of volunteer veteran players
- **BOA is NOT a game** — it's a volunteer mentoring program within Ghosts of Tabor
- **Sanctioned by**: Combat Waffle Studios (CWS), developers of Ghosts of Tabor
- **Game**: Ghosts of Tabor — VR extraction shooter
- **Official game wiki**: https://got.miraheze.org/wiki/Ghosts_of_Tabor_Wiki
- **BOA Hub Wiki**: https://boa.miraheze.org — volunteer-maintained, focuses on mentoring/tactics

## GOT Wiki BOA Supply Drop

The GoT wiki has a "BOA Supply Drop" quest — this refers to an in-game faction called
BOA within the Ghosts of Tabor game lore. It is NOT related to the BOA volunteer
mentoring program. These are two unrelated uses of the acronym.
